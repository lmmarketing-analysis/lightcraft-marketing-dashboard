Consultant's Recommendations & Key Questions

A comprehensive, proactive plan to maximize your profitability across all channels.

Strategic Rationale: The "Why"

Your SEM is your financial anchor with room to grow. Your Search Engine Marketing is a proven success. We will protect and optimize this profitable asset by reallocating budget to top performers and improving campaign efficiency.

Social Media has untapped potential. The high click rates prove people are interested in your ads, especially during sales. We must now bridge the gap between interest (clicks) and profit (sales). The problem isn't the ad platform; it's the pathway from ad to a completed sale that's broken.

The power of creative and landing pages. We need a clear understanding of which ads and which "off-ramps" (your landing pages) actually convert clicks into customers. We need to find the winning combination.

Action Plan: The "How"

A. For Social Media Marketing (SMM): The "Test & Learn" Approach

  • Reduce to a "Test & Learn" Budget: Reduce the SMM budget to a manageable amount, like $500 per month, to be used exclusively for targeted tests. This minimizes financial risk while we find a profitable formula.
  • Run Sprint Tests on Ad Creative & Audiences: We will run short, focused tests to quickly find what works. We'll create and test high-quality video ads against existing formats and test new audience segments to find your most ideal clientele.
  • Audit the "Off-Ramps" (Landing Pages): We must urgently audit the pages your ads are sending people to. Is the page loading fast? Is it easy to buy? Does the message match the ad? The campaign is failing at this stage, and fixing it is critical for conversions.

B. For Search Engine Marketing (SEM): The "Optimize & Protect" Approach

  • Demand a Conversion Tracking Audit: This is non-negotiable. Before any new campaigns, work with your agency to separate and track the value of each conversion action (e.g., phone calls, sales). This will provide you with the true data needed to measure ROI.
  • Reallocate Budget from Underperformers: Shift money away from generic and less-efficient competitor keywords to your top-performing brand and product keywords. This will make your current campaigns even more profitable.
  • Refine Ad Creative and Messaging: Even with strong performance, there's always room for improvement. Create new ad variations that highlight specific benefits like "Free Delivery" or "60% Off" to improve your click-through and conversion rates.

C. For Performance Max (PMAX): The "Strategic Pilot"

  • Pilot with a Clear Goal: Launch PMAX only after conversion tracking is fixed. Use a small budget as a test, with the goal of finding new customers who are similar to your most profitable existing customers.

Expected Outcomes & Metrics

By following this plan, you will have a more profitable, focused, and data-driven marketing strategy. The ultimate goal is to discover a profitable formula for social media while simultaneously making your core SEM channel even more efficient.

Key Metrics to Track: We will track the **Cost Per Action (CPA)** on SMM to find a winning strategy, the **Landing Page Conversion Rate** to identify broken "off-ramps," and the overall **SEM Conversion Rate and ROI** to measure increased profitability.
